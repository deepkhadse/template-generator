DMCE

Established in 1988, Datta Meghe College of Engineering (DMCE) is a renowned institution, accredited by NAAC and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, recognized by DTE & Govt. of Maharashtra and affiliated to Mumbai University.

NSS

The National Service Scheme is a central sector scheme of Government of India which provides a national platform to take part in various government led community service activities & programmes.

CSI

CSI DMCE - Student branch, established in the year 2012, conducts various workshops, seminars, technical competitions and also Technical fest in association with Computer Association for Technological Trends (CATT).

Women Development Cell

Gender Equality and Women Empowerment are the most important factors for progress of anation. Datta Meghe College of Engineering has put effort on gender sensitization by forming CollegeWomen Development Cell.
